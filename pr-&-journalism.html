PR & Journalism

This program is designed to equip students with professional-level skills in public relations, journalism, and media communication for print, digital, and broadcast platforms. Through newsroom simulations, writing labs, press event training, and real-time reporting, students build a portfolio that reflects their storytelling, strategic thinking, and communication expertise.

Eligibility Criteria
